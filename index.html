ℹ️ About / How to Play

Game Overview

This is a challenging two-player version of the classic Flappy Bird game, enhanced with health bars, power-ups, and multiple difficulties. The goal is to survive the longest or reach the end flag first.

Health: ❤️❤️❤️ ... (9 HP) — each player starts with 9 HP.

Goal: Fly through the pipes, collect coins, hearts and potions, use the magnet and reach the animated flag at the end to win. Avoid pipes and spikes.

Controls

  • Desktop: Player 1 — W (flap), S (speed boost). Player 2 — ↑ Arrow (flap), Space (speed boost).
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen P1 UP and P2 JUMP buttons. (Speed boost not available on mobile controls).

Tips

  • Collect coins (💿) and diamonds (💎) to increase your total currency — helpful for unlocks later.
  • Collect hearts and potions to restore HP or gain temporary buffs.
  • Golden heart grants temporary HP that can exceed your max for 15 seconds.
  • Magnet attracts nearby items — look for the glowing aura and magnet icon around your bird when active.
  • Moving obstacles appear sometimes — watch for pipes/spikes that move vertically or horizontally.
